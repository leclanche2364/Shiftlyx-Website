# Pricing — Shiftlyx

Shiftlyx is a personal operating system for shift workers — track fatigue, plan shifts around your life, and coordinate with your partner.

## Free
- Price: £0/month
- Features: Fatigue Score (0-100), Shift Planner (5 strategies), Recovery Coach, Preference Learning
- Limits: 1 user, no partner sync, no voice planning

## Premium Monthly
- Price: £3.99/month (billed monthly)
- Free trial: 1 month free for new users
- Features: All Free features + AI Voice Planner, Partner Sync (4 coordination modes), Fatigue Intelligence, Income Estimator, Annual Leave Optimiser

## Day One Annual
- Price: £18.99/year (billed annually, 60% off monthly equivalent)
- Free trial: 1 month free for new users
- Features: Same as Premium Monthly, locks in launch price for life
- Status: Early access — join waitlist to lock in this price

## Platform
- iOS 16+ and Android 10+
- Flutter + Supabase backend
- UK-based, UK GDPR compliant
- Payment via Apple App Store and Google Play Store
